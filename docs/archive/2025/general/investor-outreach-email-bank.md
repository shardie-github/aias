# Investor Outreach Email Bank

**Purpose:** Collection of email templates for different stages of investor outreach, from initial contact to follow-ups and updates.

## Initial Outreach Templates

### Template 1: Warm Introduction Request

**Subject:** Introduction Request: [Company Name]

**Body:**
```
Hi [Contact Name],

Hope you're doing well! I'm reaching out because I'm working on [Company Name], and I think [Investor Name] might be interested in what we're building.

We're [one-sentence description]. We've [traction metrics] and are looking to raise [amount] to [use of funds].

Would you be open to making an introduction? I'm happy to provide more information or a pitch deck if helpful.

Thanks!
[Your Name]
[Company]
[Email]
[Phone]
```

## Follow-Up Templates

### Template 1: First Follow-Up (5-7 days)

**Subject:** Re: [Original Subject]

**Body:**
```
Hi [Investor Name],

Just wanted to follow up on my previous email about [Company Name].

[Brief update if any news]

Would you be open to a quick call? I'm happy to work around your schedule.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 2: Second Follow-Up (2 weeks)

**Subject:** Re: [Original Subject] - Quick Update

**Body:**
```
Hi [Investor Name],

I know you're busy, so I'll keep this brief.

[Quick update on progress, e.g., "We've hit [new milestone]"]

If you're interested in learning more, I'm happy to hop on a call. If not, no worries at all.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### Template 3: Final Follow-Up (1 month)

**Subject:** Last follow-up - [Company Name]

**Body:**
```
Hi [Investor Name],

I know you get a lot of pitches, so I'll leave it here.

If you're ever interested in learning more about [Company Name], feel free to reach out anytime.

Best of luck with your portfolio!

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

## Tips for Effective Investor Emails

### Do's

✅ **Keep it short** - Busy investors, respect their time
✅ **Be specific** - Include metrics, traction, clear ask
✅ **Personalize** - Reference their portfolio, interests
✅ **Follow up** - But don't pester (3 follow-ups max)
✅ **Be professional** - But authentic and genuine

### Don'ts

❌ **Don't send mass emails** - Personalize each one
❌ **Don't oversell** - Be honest about challenges
❌ **Don't be pushy** - Respect their time and decisions
❌ **Don't forget to follow up** - But don't overdo it
❌ **Don't ignore rejection** - Respond politely

## Email Timing

**Best Times to Send:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 9 AM - 11 AM or 2 PM - 4 PM
- Avoid Mondays (busy) and Fridays (checking out)

**Follow-Up Timing:**
- First follow-up: 5-7 days
- Second follow-up: 2 weeks
- Final follow-up: 1 month

## Tracking

**Track in CRM:**
- Email sent date
- Response received
- Meeting scheduled
- Status (interested, not interested, pending)
- Next follow-up date

- Airtable/Notion CRM
