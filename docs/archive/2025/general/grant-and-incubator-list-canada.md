# Canadian Grants & Incubator List (2025)

**Purpose:** Comprehensive list of active grant programs, incubators, and funding opportunities available to Canadian startups and small businesses in 2025.

## Government Grants

### Federal Programs

#### 1. Industrial Research Assistance Program (IRAP)

**Organization:** National Research Council of Canada (NRC)
**Type:** Grant
**Amount:** Up to $500K CAD
**Eligibility:** Small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs) with < 500 employees
**Focus:** Technology R&D, innovation
**Deadline:** Rolling intake
**Website:** https://nrc-cnrc.gc.ca/eng/irap/index.html

**Notes:**
- Can take 3-6 months for approval
- Requires technical merit and commercial potential
- Consultants available to help with applications

---

#### 2. Scientific Research and Experimental Development (SR&ED)

**Organization:** Canada Revenue Agency (CRA)
**Type:** Tax credit
**Amount:** Up to 35% of eligible R&D expenses (refundable for small businesses)
**Eligibility:** Companies conducting R&D in Canada
**Focus:** Scientific research and experimental development
**Deadline:** Tax filing deadline (18 months after tax year-end)
**Website:** https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/scientific-research-experimental-development-tax-incentive-program.html

**Notes:**
- Most significant R&D incentive in Canada
- Can be retroactive
- Consider hiring SR&ED consultant

---

#### 3. Canada Digital Adoption Program (CDAP)

**Organization:** Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada (ISED)
**Type:** Grant + loan
**Amount:** Up to $100K grant + $100K loan
**Eligibility:** Small and medium-sized businesses
**Focus:** Digital transformation
**Deadline:** Rolling (program may be winding down, check status)
**Website:** https://ised-isde.canada.ca/site/canada-digital-adoption-program/en

**Notes:**
- Boost Your Business Technology stream
- Grow Your Business Online stream
- Requires digital advisor engagement

---

#### 4. Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF)

**Organization:** Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada (ISED)
**Type:** Grant/loan
**Amount:** $10M+ CAD
**Eligibility:** Large-scale projects, significant economic impact
**Focus:** Large-scale R&D and commercialization
**Deadline:** Rolling intake
**Website:** https://ised-isde.canada.ca/site/strategic-innovation-fund/en

**Notes:**
- For larger companies/projects
- Minimum project size typically $10M+
- Longer application process

---

#### 5. CanExport Program

**Organization:** Trade Commissioner Service
**Type:** Grant
**Amount:** Up to $75K CAD
**Eligibility:** SMEs looking to export
**Focus:** Export market development
**Deadline:** Rolling intake
**Website:** https://www.tradecommissioner.gc.ca/canexport/index.aspx

**Notes:**
- For export-focused businesses
- Reimbursement-based (50% of eligible costs)
- Requires export plan

## Incubators & Accelerators

### National Programs

#### 1. Techstars

**Locations:** Toronto, Montreal, Vancouver (rotating)
**Type:** Accelerator
**Program:** 13-week program
**Equity:** ~6% for $120K USD
**Deadline:** Various cohorts throughout year
**Website:** https://www.techstars.com/

**Notes:**
- Highly competitive
- Strong network and mentorship
- Demo day at end

---

#### 2. Y Combinator

**Location:** Remote (occasional Canadian presence)
**Type:** Accelerator
**Program:** 3-month program
**Equity:** ~7% for $125K USD
**Deadline:** Twice yearly (winter/summer)
**Website:** https://www.ycombinator.com/

- Remote-friendly
- Extremely competitive

## Application Tips

### General Grant Application Tips

1. **Start Early** - Applications can take weeks to prepare
2. **Read Guidelines Carefully** - Follow all requirements exactly
3. **Get Help** - Consultants can improve success rates
4. **Be Specific** - Detailed proposals perform better
5. **Demonstrate Impact** - Show economic/job creation potential
6. **Have Matching Funds** - Many grants require matching
7. **Follow Up** - Don't be afraid to check status

### SR&ED Specific Tips

1. **Document Everything** - Keep detailed R&D records
2. **Hire SR&ED Consultant** - Can significantly increase claim
3. **File on Time** - 18-month deadline is strict
4. **Claim All Eligible Expenses** - Salaries, materials, contractors
5. **Technical Write-up** - Clear explanation of R&D work

### Incubator Application Tips

1. **Show Traction** - Even early traction helps
2. **Clear Value Prop** - Articulate what you're building
3. **Team Matters** - Show strong founding team
4. **Market Size** - Demonstrate large opportunity
5. **Coachability** - Show willingness to learn
6. **Network** - Get warm introductions if possible
