## Cost Optimization Tips

### Free Tier First
- ✅ Use free tiers (Vercel, Resend, GitHub, Google Analytics)
- ✅ Upgrade only when necessary (scale-based)

### Minimize Fixed Costs
- ✅ Start with minimal infrastructure (Supabase Pro CAD $25/month)
- ✅ Add services only when needed (scaling)

### Marketing Efficiency
- ✅ Focus on organic growth (content, social media, communities)
- ✅ Use paid advertising only when ROI positive (CAC < LTV)
