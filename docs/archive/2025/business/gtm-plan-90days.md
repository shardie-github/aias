# GTM Plan — 90 Days — AIAS Platform

**Launch Date:** March 1, 2024  
**Duration:** 90 days (Q1 2024)  
**Goal:** 500 beta users, 20% paid conversion (CAD $4,900 MRR)

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Days 0-7)

### Day 0-2: Preparation
- ✅ **Landing Page:** Launch waitlist landing page (waitlist.aias-platform.com)
- ✅ **Social Media:** Set up Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram accounts
- ✅ **Email List:** Collect email addresses (waitlist sign-ups)
- ✅ **Press Kit:** Prepare launch press kit (media assets, one-pager)

### Day 3-5: Announcement
- ✅ **Email Announcement:** Send waitlist announcement email (to existing contacts)
- ✅ **Social Media:** Post waitlist announcement (Twitter, LinkedIn)
- ✅ **Communities:** Post in relevant communities (Indie Hackers, Product Hunt, Reddit)
- ✅ **Press Outreach:** Reach out to 10 tech bloggers/journalists

### Day 6-7: Waitlist Building
- ✅ **Content:** Publish 2 blog posts (problem-solution fit, Canadian SMB automation)
- ✅ **Social Media:** Daily social media posts (tips, updates, behind-the-scenes)
- ✅ **Email Follow-Up:** Follow up with waitlist sign-ups (thank you, updates)

**Target:** 200 waitlist sign-ups

---

## Phase 2: Early Access (Days 8-30)

### Week 2-3: Beta Launch
- ✅ **Beta Invites:** Send beta invites to first 100 waitlist users
- ✅ **Onboarding:** Onboarding emails, tutorials, support
- ✅ **Feedback:** Collect feedback (surveys, interviews, support tickets)
- ✅ **Iteration:** Quick iterations based on feedback

### Week 4: Lighthouse Users
- ✅ **Lighthouse Program:** Select 10 lighthouse users (active, engaged)
- ✅ **Case Studies:** Interview lighthouse users, collect testimonials
- ✅ **Referrals:** Ask lighthouse users for referrals (referral program)

**Target:** 100 active beta users

---

## Phase 3: Public Launch (Days 31-60)

### Week 5-6: Public Launch
- ✅ **Product Hunt:** Launch on Product Hunt (target: top 5)
- ✅ **Press Release:** Press release to tech media (TechCrunch, BetaKit, etc.)
- ✅ **Social Media:** Launch announcement (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram)
- ✅ **Email Campaign:** Launch email to waitlist (beta users, prospects)

### Week 7-8: Scale
- ✅ **Content Marketing:** Publish 4 blog posts (Canadian SMB automation, case studies)
- ✅ **Social Media:** Daily social media posts (tips, updates, user stories)
- ✅ **Paid Ads:** Launch Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads (target: Canadian SMBs)
- ✅ **Partnerships:** Reach out to Shopify, Wave for partnerships

**Target:** 300 active users, 50 paid conversions

---

## Phase 4: Growth (Days 61-90)

### Week 9-10: Optimization
- ✅ **Conversion Optimization:** Optimize landing page, pricing page (A/B testing)
- ✅ **Email Campaigns:** Nurture email campaigns (onboarding, feature announcements)
- ✅ **Community Engagement:** Engage in communities (Reddit, Indie Hackers, LinkedIn)
- ✅ **Content:** Publish 4 blog posts (advanced workflows, integrations)

### Week 11-12: Scale & Retention
- ✅ **Retention:** Focus on retention (onboarding improvements, support)
- ✅ **Referrals:** Launch referral program (referral incentives)
- ✅ **Partnerships:** Expand partnerships (Shopify App Store, Wave integrations)
- ✅ **Content:** Publish 4 blog posts (success stories, best practices)

**Target:** 500 active users, 100 paid conversions (CAD $4,900 MRR)
