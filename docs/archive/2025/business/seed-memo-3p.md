## Page 1: Problem, Solution, Market

### The Problem

Canadian solo entrepreneurs and small businesses waste 10+ hours per week on repetitive tasks that AI can automate. Current solutions are either:
- **Too expensive:** Enterprise tools cost CAD $150-500/month (Zapier, Make)
- **Too complex:** Setup takes 2+ hours (technical expertise required)
- **Not Canadian:** Missing Canadian integrations (Shopify, Wave, RBC)

**Market Size:**
- 2M+ Canadian solo operators (addressable market)
- CAD $450M Canadian SMB automation market (21% of total market)
- 42% of Canadian SMBs plan to adopt AI in 2024 (StatCan)

### The Solution

AIAS Platform is a no-code AI agent builder that lets Canadian businesses create, deploy, and manage custom AI agents and automation workflows without technical expertise.

**Key Features:**
- **No-Code Builder:** Drag-and-drop interface (30-minute setup)
- **Canadian Integrations:** Shopify, Wave, Stripe CAD, RBC, TD (20+ integrations)
- **AI Agents:** Context-aware AI agents (not just workflows)
- **Affordable Pricing:** CAD $49/month (vs. $150+ competitors)

**Differentiation:**
- **Canadian-First:** PIPEDA-compliant, CAD pricing, Canadian integrations
- **Affordable:** Lowest price (CAD $49 vs. $50-300+ competitors)
- **AI-Powered:** AI agents (not just rule-based automation)

### Market Opportunity

**TAM (Total Addressable Market):** CAD $2.1B (Canadian automation software market)  
**SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market):** CAD $450M (Canadian SMB automation market)  
**SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market):** CAD $45M (1% market share by Year 3)
