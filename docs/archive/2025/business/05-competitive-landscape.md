## Competitive Advantages (Moats)

### 1. Canadian-First Approach
- **What:** 20+ Canadian integrations (Shopify, Wave, RBC, TD, Interac)
- **Why it matters:** Competitors focus on US/EU markets; we serve Canada specifically
- **Durability:** Medium (competitors can add Canadian integrations)

### 2. Affordable Pricing
- **What:** CAD $49/month (vs. CAD $50-300+ competitors)
- **Why it matters:** Solo operators price-sensitive; we're most affordable
- **Durability:** Medium (competitors can lower prices)

### 3. AI Agents (Not Just Workflows)
- **What:** Context-aware AI agents (vs. rule-based automation)
- **Why it matters:** AI understands business context; workflows are generic
- **Durability:** High (requires AI expertise; hard to replicate)

### 4. Fast Setup (30 Minutes)
- **What:** Pre-built templates + no-code builder = 30-minute setup
- **Why it matters:** Competitors take 2+ hours; we reduce friction
- **Durability:** Medium (competitors can improve UX)

### 5. PIPEDA Compliance
- **What:** Canadian data residency + PIPEDA-compliant privacy policy
- **Why it matters:** Canadian businesses prefer Canadian compliance
- **Durability:** High (regulatory advantage; hard to change)
