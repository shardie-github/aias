## Monthly Traction Metrics

### User Metrics
- **Total Users:** [Number] (cumulative sign-ups)
- **Active Users (WAU):** [Number] (weekly active users)
- **Active Users (DAU):** [Number] (daily active users)
- **Paid Users:** [Number] (paid subscriptions)

### Acquisition Metrics
- **New Sign-Ups:** [Number] (new sign-ups this month)
- **Activation Rate:** [%] (% of sign-ups who create first workflow within 7 days)
- **Paid Conversion Rate:** [%] (% of free users who convert to paid within 30 days)

### Engagement Metrics
- **7-Day Retention:** [%] (% of users who return 7 days after sign-up)
- **30-Day Retention:** [%] (% of users who return 30 days after sign-up)
- **Workflow Runs:** [Number] (total workflow executions this month)
- **Average Workflows per User:** [Number] (average workflows created per user)

### Revenue Metrics
- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue):** CAD $[Amount] (monthly recurring revenue)
- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue):** CAD $[Amount] (annual recurring revenue)
- **ARPU (Average Revenue Per User):** CAD $[Amount] (average revenue per user)
- **Churn Rate:** [%] (% of paid users who cancel per month)

### Growth Metrics
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** CAD $[Amount] (cost to acquire a new paid customer)
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** CAD $[Amount] (average lifetime value of a paid customer)
- **Payback Period:** [Months] (time to recover CAC)
- **Magic Number:** [Number] (LTV ÷ CAC)
