# Privacy Policy Draft

**Status:** DRAFT - Requires Legal Review  
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-XX  
**Effective Date:** TBD

## Plain Language Summary

We respect your privacy. This policy explains:
- What data we collect and why
- How we use and protect your data
- Your rights and how to exercise them
- How to contact us about privacy concerns

**TL;DR:** We only collect data necessary to provide the service. We don't sell your data. You can export or delete your data anytime.

## Detailed Privacy Policy

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address, password (hashed), display name
- **Profile Information:** Optional profile details, preferences
- **Content:** User-generated content, posts, comments, journal entries
- **Billing Information:** Processed by Stripe, we do not store full payment details

#### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, device information
- **Telemetry:** Application performance metrics, error logs
- **Guardian Events:** Privacy-relevant events (see Guardian documentation)

#### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **OAuth Providers:** If you sign in with Google/GitHub/etc., we receive basic profile information
- **Analytics:** Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (if analytics consent granted)

### 2. How We Use Your Information

#### 2.1 Service Provision

- Authenticate your account
- Provide core features and functionality
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Deliver support and respond to inquiries

**Lawful Basis:** Contract (GDPR Article 6(1)(b))

#### 2.2 Service Improvement

- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Fix bugs and optimize performance
- Conduct security monitoring and threat detection

**Lawful Basis:** Legitimate Interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

#### 2.3 Communications

- Send transactional emails (account updates, password resets)
- Send marketing emails (only with consent)
- Respond to support requests

**Lawful Basis:** Consent (marketing) or Contract (transactional)

#### 2.4 Legal Compliance

- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to lawful requests from authorities
- Protect rights and safety

**Lawful Basis:** Legal Obligation (GDPR Article 6(1)(c))

### 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

#### 3.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

#### 3.2 Service Providers

We share data with trusted service providers who assist in operations:

- **Supabase:** Hosting, database, authentication (US/EU)
- **Vercel:** Application hosting, CDN (US/EU)
- **Stripe:** Payment processing (US)
- **Resend:** Email delivery (if enabled)

All processors are bound by Data Processing Agreements (DPAs).

#### 3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.

#### 3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change.

### 4. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and European Union. We rely on:

- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)** for transfers to processors
- **Adequacy Decisions** where applicable
- **Supabase EU regions** for EU-based processing (if configured)

### 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Rationale |
|-----------|------------------|-----------|
| Account Data | Until account deletion | Service provision |
| Usage Data | 12 months | Service improvement |
| Content Data | Until deletion request | User control |
| Telemetry Data | 90 days | Privacy monitoring |
| Billing Data | 7 years | Legal requirement (tax) |
| Support Data | 3 years | Contract records |

### 6. Your Rights (Data Subject Rights)

#### 6.1 Access

Request a copy of your personal data. Visit `/account/export` (coming soon).

#### 6.2 Rectification

Correct inaccurate data through your account settings or by contacting us.

#### 6.3 Erasure

Request deletion of your account and data. Visit `/account/settings` or email privacy@example.com.

#### 6.4 Portability

Export your data in a machine-readable format (JSON). Visit `/account/export` (coming soon).

#### 6.5 Restriction

Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances.

#### 6.6 Objection

Object to processing based on legitimate interests. Contact privacy@example.com.

#### 6.7 Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for analytics or marketing at any time through account preferences.

**Response Time:** We aim to respond within 30 days, as required by GDPR and PIPEDA.

### 7. Children's Data

Our service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

### 8. Security Measures

- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 in transit, encryption at rest
- **Access Control:** Row-Level Security (RLS) policies
- **Authentication:** Secure password hashing, MFA available
- **Monitoring:** Guardian privacy monitoring, security audits
- **Incident Response:** Security incident response plan in place

See `docs/trust/SECURITY.md` for details.

### 9. Cookies and Tracking

#### 9.1 Essential Cookies

Required for core functionality (authentication, session management). Cannot be disabled.

#### 9.2 Analytics Cookies

Optional, used to improve the service. Requires consent. Can be disabled in preferences.

#### 9.3 Third-Party Cookies

We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising.
